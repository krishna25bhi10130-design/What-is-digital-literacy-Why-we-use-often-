Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)
General Safety Tips
    Use strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers, symbols) and never reuse them
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email and social media accounts
Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages
Always verify unknown calls or messages before sharing any personal information
Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts
Log out from shared or public computers after use
Be careful about what you post online to protect your privacy and reputation
  Financial / UPI Safety Tips
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank
Do not approve unknown payment requests in apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm
Always double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before sending money
Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources
  Report Cybercrime
Report incidents at: National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for immediate assistance
