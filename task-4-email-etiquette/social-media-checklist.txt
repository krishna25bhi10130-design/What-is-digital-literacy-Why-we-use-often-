Part B – Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
 Do’s
Think before posting anything online
Maintain respectful and polite communication
Protect your personal information and privacy
Share accurate and verified information
Use social media for learning and networking
Don’ts
Do not share fake news or misinformation
Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
Do not overshare personal details
Avoid cyberbullying or negative comments
Do not damage your digital reputation
